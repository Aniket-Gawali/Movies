Night Life In Pune


More Informaction About Night Life In Pune


There are over a hundred pubs and night-clubs in Pune, and it is one among the very few cities of India where pubs stretch their shutdown time till up to 4 AM. A weekend getaway would be perfect for enjoying the relaxed vibe of the place, dance your worries away and re-fuel your exhausted self with some delicious food and cocktails.

Places :

1.High Spirits



If you are a pocket-friendly reveller, this place for nightlife in Pune is undoubtedly the place for you! They offer some of the best drinks at affordable prices. When you decide to get started with your nightlife along with your buddies and wish to have your pockets yet full, this is the place to be.


Address :


Where in Pune : 35, A 1, Near ABC Farms, KP Annexe, Mundhwa Road, Mundhwa
Highlights : Frequent live gigs and off-beat music
Approx. cost for two : INR 1500


2.Mi A Mi


Mi A Mi, a club designed like an underground garage, shelters a large dance floor! Mi A Mi often hires the best DJ in town to play some of the top dance music. Party addicts frequent this club in Pune, and if you are one of them, this is your choice!


Address :


Where in Pune : JW Marriott, Senapati Bapat Road, Pune
Highlights : White Bed Lounge Section to talk away the night with your loved ones
Approx. cost for two : INR 2500
